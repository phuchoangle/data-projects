Data Collection & Methodology

Data Source: Investment data was aggregated from Messari and CryptoRank, covering publicly disclosed funding rounds in the blockchain and cryptocurrency sector from November 2024 through April 2025.

Dataset: The dataset contains 338 unique funding rounds across 326 projects, involving 1,298 unique investors. Each row represents an investor-deal pair, with 2,364 total records after cleaning.

Data Processing: Category labels were standardized (merging ecosystem-specific tags into parent categories). Fundraise and pre-valuation figures are in millions USD. Duplicate project-date entries were removed to count unique deals. Co-investment relationships are inferred from shared participation in the same funding round.

Limitations: This dataset covers publicly disclosed rounds only. Undisclosed or stealth-mode investments are not represented. Some rounds may have additional undisclosed participants. Fundraise figures are self-reported and may include tokens/equity mix.

Analysis Tools: Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, networkx), Chart.js for interactive dashboard. All code and data are available in the accompanying Jupyter notebook.
