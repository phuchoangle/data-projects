Strategic Recommendations

Market Positioning

Optimize for Ethereum, Arbitrum, and 2-3 rotating top chains. These capture 77-83% of all addressable volume. A long-tail chain strategy is not efficient.

Messaging Opportunity

43.5% of volume now flows through messaging layers. Interoperability infrastructure, not just token bridges, is the growth vector for 2025-2026.

Revenue Sizing

At 3 bps the TAM is $77M annualized. Messaging alone is $33M. At 5 bps the total rises to $128M. Actual capture depends on competitive share.

Growth Targets

Arbitrum (+36.9%) and Sei (+34.1%) are accelerating. Sui and Blast are declining sharply. Hyperlane is the breakout messaging protocol.

Risk Factors

Heavy concentration on Ethereum/Arbitrum. Daily volume CV > 0.5 implies high volatility. 76+ competing protocols mean fee compression pressure.
