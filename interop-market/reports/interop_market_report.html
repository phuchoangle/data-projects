Strategic Recommendations

Scale Integration Velocity

At current rates the coverage gap widens in absolute terms every year. Protocols must automate chain onboarding or develop permissionless integration frameworks to outpace ecosystem growth.

Target L2 / L3 Rollups

131 L2s and 29 L3s are the fastest-growing segment. Hyperlane's modular, permissionless architecture is best positioned here. Targeting OP Stack and Arbitrum Orbit frameworks unlocks batched integrations across dozens of chains simultaneously.

Differentiate on Speed

313 unserviced chains are an addressable first-mover opportunity. Protocols that can integrate a new chain in hours rather than weeks can capture the long tail of appchains before defaults are set by competitors.

Market Sizing

313 unserviced chains represent 62.9% of total addressable market. If each chain generates even $1M/year in bridge fee volume, the uncaptured opportunity exceeds $313M annually — growing to $620M+ by 2027.

Provider Consolidation Risk

Hyperlane's 2024 surge (+88 chains in a single year) signals an emerging dominant player. Providers that cannot match this pace risk being locked out of newly launched chains as early integration defaults become sticky.
